<h2><a id="user-content-preamble" class="anchor" aria-hidden="true" href="#preamble"><svg class="octicon octicon-link" viewBox="0 0 16 16" version="1.1" width="16" height="16" aria-hidden="true"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Preamble</h2>
<p>As time has shown, most general <em>"Where should I start?"</em> or <em>"What do I need to get started?"</em>-type questions asked in various outlets can be answered with a limited amount of complementary information. This information is currently nowhere to be found except in the heads of active devs, or scattered in <a href="https://documentation.b2c.commercecloud.salesforce.com/DOC1/index.jsp" rel="nofollow">documentation</a>, <a href="https://xchange.demandware.com/" rel="nofollow">XChange posts</a> and <a href="https://support-demandware.force.com/customer/_ui/knowledge/ui/KnowledgeHome" rel="nofollow">Salesforce support platform wiki entries</a>.</p>
